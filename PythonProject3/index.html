Professional Code of Ethics Awareness Survey

Welcome to our survey on the Professional Code of Ethics for Accountancy students.

We are students from Mapúa Malayan Digital College (a college under Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna). This survey is part of our course requirement for MO-HUM039 – Ethics, Term 3, SY 2024–2025, General Education – A1103.

Your participation will help us understand how well incoming 2nd-year accountancy students are familiar with the ethical foundations essential in their profession. Your insights will contribute to developing better educational strategies that promote integrity, responsibility, and ethical practice among future accountants.

This survey will take approximately 4 minutes to complete. Please answer each question as honestly and thoughtfully as possible. All responses are strictly anonymous and will be used solely for educational and research purposes. Thank you!
